About Me

Bridging Theory &
Industrial Practice

I am Jeric, an Electrical Technology student at BISU Calape (Bohol Island State University – Calape Campus), currently in my 2nd year of the Bindtech-Electtech program. My journey in electrical technology is driven by a deep passion for creating safe, efficient, and innovative electrical systems.

From residential and commercial wiring to motor controls, PLCs, and Arduino programming, I thrive at the intersection of theory and hands-on application. My highlight project — a complete 1.8KW Solar System Installation — reflects my commitment to renewable energy and sustainable solutions.

Beyond electrical work, I hold an NC II in Tailoring, showcasing my versatility and discipline across technical fields.

Aiming to become a licensed electrical engineer and renewable energy specialist, contributing to a smarter, greener future.
